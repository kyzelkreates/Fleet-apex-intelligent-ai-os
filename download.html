How to install on Android
1

Download the APK

Tap the Android APK button above. The file will download to your phone's Downloads folder.

2

Allow unknown sources

Go to Settings → Security (or Apps → Special access) and enable Install unknown apps for your browser or file manager.

3

Open the file

Open your Downloads folder, tap the .apk file, and tap Install. Takes about 10 seconds.

4

Open Fleet Apex

Find the app on your home screen or app drawer. Tap it to launch — it's ready to use immediately.
