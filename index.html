Our Story

We didn't set out to
take on the giants.

It started with frustration. Not the quiet kind — the kind that builds up over years of watching drivers struggle with clunky apps, fleet managers drown in spreadsheets, and six-figure software bills deliver less than a well-built mobile app could do for free.

The big players — the Volvos, the Samsaras, the Geotabs of the world — they've built empires. Thousands of engineers, hundreds of millions in funding, decades of brand trust. We have nothing but respect for what they've built.

But here's the thing: those platforms were designed for enterprise procurement cycles, not for the owner-operator running 12 vans, or the regional haulier trying to stay compliant without hiring a dedicated compliance officer. The software is built for the sale, not the driver.

So in 2021, we started building something different. Not bigger. Better.

We started with a single question: "What would fleet software look like if it was built entirely around the people who use it every day?" The answer took five years, thousands of hours of late nights, complete rebuilds, and more cups of coffee than we care to admit.

Fleet Apex Intelligent AI OS isn't trying to replace those companies. We're not chasing their customers. We're building for the fleets they forgot about — and doing it in a way that makes them quietly wonder why they didn't think of it first.

"We're not trying to be the biggest. We're trying to be the one you'd actually recommend to a mate who just bought their first van."
