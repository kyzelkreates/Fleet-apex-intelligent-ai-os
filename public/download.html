How to install the APK on Android

Tap Download

Press the green or purple download button above. The .apk file downloads to your device.

2

Allow installs

Go to Settings → Security → Install unknown apps and allow your browser app.

3

Open the file

Open your Downloads folder, tap the .apk file, then tap Install.

4

Launch

Find Fleet Apex on your home screen or app drawer and tap to open.
